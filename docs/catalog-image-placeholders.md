## Photography guidance

- Photograph products on neutral charcoal, gray or clean industrial backgrounds.
- Keep carbon weave sharp and correctly exposed; avoid crushed blacks.
- Leave negative space for captions where possible.
- Do not stretch low-resolution images.
- Use consistent color temperature and similar contrast across the catalog.
- Photograph product labels separately so batch or confidential information can be removed.
- Obtain permission before showing customer parts, branded packaging or identifiable staff.
